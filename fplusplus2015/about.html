Why Recycling?

To save the Earth! Each year Americans produce 254 MILLION TONS of trash. In 2013, 34% of this was recycled, saving roughly 1.1 QUADRILLION BTU of energy or enough energy to power 10 MILLION homes for a year. Recycling 1 ton of paper saves the energy equivalent to 166 gallons of gasoline! Join us in this Green Revolution, and recycle today!
